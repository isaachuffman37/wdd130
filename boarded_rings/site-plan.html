Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a user friendly interface for those that want to purchase a ring. It will provide information on how each ring is made and also allow people to choose and purchase a ring that they love. It will also provide a way for people to contact us for custom requests.

Audience

This website will be directed towards a younger audience (12-25 years old). It is the websites goal to catch the interest of skaters and the interest of people who want to add style to their rustic fashion.
