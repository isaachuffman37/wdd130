Overview

Purpose

This website will allow white-whater sport lovers to easily explore the options of rafting down the snake river. They will be able to schedule a time to rent boats (and other necessary safety equipment), book a guide, and explore what path they want to take. This website will build revenue by making the process of rafting a lot smoother. It will also promote some extra equipment that the company can rent out for extra charge, bringing in even more revenue.

Audience

We are trying to extend the white-water rafting experience to all people who love the thrill of adventure. We target people of all levels of experience by providing the option of a guide and providing a map that grades different parts of the river from "Beginner" to "Advanced". We try to appeal mostly to groups and familys by offering discounts for equipment for groups over 8 people, seniors (over 65 years old), and any children under 12. We know that familys with young children will love the options of having a guide and also the safety courses that we offer. This site will make those options very easy to access. It is hard for a family to accurately and efficiently plan a safe rafting experince that everyone will love, but with this site, this process will be much easier.
